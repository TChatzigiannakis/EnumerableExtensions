EnumerableExtensions

Extension methods for IEnumerable<T>.

View project on GitHub

EnumerableExtensions is a C# library containing extensions for IEnumerable<T>. Some methods are new and some are simply more convenient overloads of existing methods. Most of them follow the spirit of LINQ, in that they often take delegates (like lambdas) and they use lazy evaluation where possible.

The library is written and maintained by Theodoros Chatzigiannakis.

It is distributed as free software, covered by the MPLv2. If you would like to obtain it under different licensing terms, feel free to contact me.
